Commands:

Make addon:
ee make:addon emoji_reactions --description='Allow users to react to channel entries with emojis' --version='1.0.0' --author='Matt Johnson' --author-url='basicblog.test'

Fix lang
Fix icon

Make Emoji Model and EmojiReaction:
ee make:model Emoji --addon=emoji_reactions
ee make:model EmojiReaction --addon=emoji_reactions

Make both model migrations:
ee make:migration --name='Create emoji table' --create --location=emoji_reactions --table=emoji
ee make:migration --name='Create Emoji Reaction table' --create --location=emoji_reactions --table=emoji_reaction

Make two template tags:
ee make:template-tag --addon=emoji_reactions
    ReactButton
    ListReactions

Make Action:
ee make:action RecordReaction --addon=emoji_reactions

Make a Control Panel Route
ee make:cp-route

Make a widget
ee make:widget

Make a prolet
ee make:prolet
